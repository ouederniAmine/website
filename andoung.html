ANDOUNG Sawn Timber AD CBM/MONTH 2METER UP 10 CONTAINER OF 40 FEET /MONTH

The tropical species andoung is a light to medium-heavy, soft to medium-hard wood. It is not durable and is best used inside with no risk of dampness. The sapwood is not clearly demarcated, so it is necessary to treat the wood against dry wood borers.

It is used in the plywood industry due to its good peeling.
